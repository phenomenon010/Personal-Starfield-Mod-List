Multiverse Playthrough Plan

The Starfield engine has its limitations, especially when trying to run massive, heavy load orders all at once. Staging these mods across distinct Unity jumps is a fantastic way to work within those technical boundaries while ensuring peak stability.

Beyond performance, this method takes the core multiverse theme that attracted us to the game and pushes it a step further: by altering the quests, points of interest, and world-spaces in each iteration, we guarantee that every time you step through the Unity, it truly feels like exploring a fresh, new universe. The tables below map out exactly which mods are active during each specific jump.
