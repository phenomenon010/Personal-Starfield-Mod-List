⚠️ The Engine Limit: Worldspace Overloads

Starfield’s engine can only handle a limited number of worldspace edits at once.

Big overhaul mods — like New Atlantis Awaits, Akila City Rises, and Neon Veins — rewrite huge parts of the game world: city layouts, interiors, navmesh, lighting, encounters, and streaming cells.

Quest mods and POI mods also rewrite worldspaces, interiors, and cell data. When you try to run ALL of these together, the engine gets overloaded. That causes:

  • 🛑 Stutters
  • 🛑 Broken interiors
  • 🛑 Missing NPCs
  • 🛑 Corrupted navmesh
  • 🛑 Infinite loading
  • 🛑 And full game crashes

💡 The Solution: Rotation

By activating only ONE major overhaul per Unity universe, the engine only has to stream one set of city edits at a time. Quest mods and POI mods stay stable. Creations stay stable. Streaming stays stable. Your save stays healthy.

And the best part? Every Unity jump becomes a fresh universe with a new city overhaul — new visuals, new atmosphere, new exploration.

Rotation = stability + variety + endless content.
