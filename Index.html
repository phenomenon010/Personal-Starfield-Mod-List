FableFire's Starfield Mod List

Building a stable, heavily modified load order requires precision. Whether integrating new story arcs, adjusting mesh weighting, editing NIF files for custom assets, or scripting Kismet sequences, these carefully selected mods offer an expansive and stable foundation for a transformed universe.

Custom INI Configs Mod Slot List (Weapons/Gear)
Music and Radio
WSTR Radio
by xXGRIMNIRXx
Listen to three DJs: Ghost (morning show), Misty Graves (afternoon/night show), and Ron "Hal" Vega (third shift). Each has their own commercials & news and can be played in-game using an included radio mod.
Must-Haves for New Players or New Game
Shattered Space Delayed Start
A must-have for all new players. Shattered Space can easily be triggered at any point early on and has a recommended level of 35+. This prevents early shredding for new games (NOT NG+).
Terran Armada Delayed
by Aurelianis
Delays Terran Armada based on the selected player level or main quest progress in Gameplay Options, with an option to delay indefinitely.

⚠️ Special Mod Notes & Conflict Warnings

Gangs All Here

  • Main Story Quests: Use only ONE companion to avoid breaking quest scripts.
  • Companion Quests: Conflicts can cause progression issues; limit to 1 companion or lock extra companions in mod settings.
  • General Play: Keep active companions to two or three max for stability.

Upgraded Companions

Must be loaded BEFORE "The Gang's All Here" in your load order.

Legacy's End - Wild Card

  • Companion Limit: Bring ONLY 1 companion to prevent narrative breaks.
  • Dark Universe - Crossfire Conflict: Make a save immediately before grav jumping to the designated space station. If space encounters load on top of the friendly fleet, reload to prevent progression issues.
  • Fleet Mods: Using Watchtower or Fleet Commander will make space combat extremely easy.
  • Useful Brigs: Do not subdue named quest NPCs. Release or turn in prisoners before advancing to the space station to prevent brig bugs.

Chunks Noir

Known Issue: Due to a change to Athena Tower Elevators, pre-established saves require a location reset on New Atlantis. Travel to Venus and wait 10 in-game hours to fix elevator alignment issues.
