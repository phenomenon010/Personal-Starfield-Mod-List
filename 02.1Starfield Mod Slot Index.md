# Starfield Equipment Mod Database (2026 Version 244)

#### Spacesuit Modifications

##### Base Mods

###### Slot 1

**Ballistic Shielding:** Increases physical damage resistance.
**EM Shielding:** Increases Electromagnetic resistance.
**Energy Shielding:** Increases Energy resistance.

###### *Slot 1 **(Additional Armor Mods)***

**Improved Ballistic Shielding:** greatly increases ballistic resistance.

**EM+Energy Shielding:** increases both energy and EM resistances.

**Vigilance Slot 1: Poly-Aramid Weave:** Weaves an advanced poly-aramid fiber layer into the undersuit that greatly improves resistance to impact damage but is very heavy.

**Vigilance Slot 1: Artificial Muscle:** Installs a thin artificial muscle layer that responds to the wearers movements in order to increase strength and speed but it is very expensive to produce.

**Vigilance Slot 1: Energy Dissipation:** Installs a Caelumite based layer in the undersuit that helps dissipate incoming energy weapon damage as well as improving thermal and radiation resistance.



###### Slot 2

**Explosive Shielding:** Reduces damage taken from explosives.
**Optimized Servos:** Reduces Oxygen cost for sprinting.
**Auto-Medic:** Automatically use a Med Pack when hit and health is below 25%, once every 60 seconds.

###### *Slot 2 **(Additional Armor Mods)***

**Thermal/Corrosive/Radiation/Airborne Shielding:** increases resistance to corresponding environmental damage.

**Vigilance Slot 2: ERA:** Fits the armor plating with a type of old Earth style armor that absorbs a massive amount of damage but is single use and won't work when the suit is damaged in any way (below 99% Health).



###### Slot 3

**Heavy Shielding:** Increases all overarching damage resistances.
**Exo Servos:** Increases melee damage output.

###### *Slot 3 **(Additional Armor Mods)***

**Chem Distribution System:** increases all Chems duration.

**Composite Materials:** reduces weight.

**Hazard Protection:** increases resistance to all types of environmental damage.

**Vigilance Slot 3: Aim Stabilizer:** Equips the armor with a suite of systems that assist with aiming down a scope or sight by compensating for natural movement or 'sway'.



###### Slot 4

**Pocketed:** Increases overall carrying capacity.
**Gravitic Composites:** Reduces detection from enemies, enhancing stealth.

###### *Slot 4 **(Additional Armor Mods)***

**Fortified Servos:** increases jump height.

**Life Support:** decreases change of getting an affliction.

**Internal Dampeners:** increases stagger resistance.

**Vigilance Slot 4: Climate Control:** Fits the suit with more complex climate systems that help maintain the users comfort and safety in harsh environments.

**Vigilance Slot 4: Emergency Shield:** Reverse engineered from the single use deployable shields this suit modification will automatically deploy a shield to protect the user in emergency situations (below 30% Health) but has a cooldown period between activations.

**Vigilance Slot 4: Damage Mitigation:** Installs a prototype shield generator that absorbs some incoming damage until the suit is badly damaged (below 25% Health).

#### Weapon Modifications

##### Base Mods

###### Barrel

**Standard Barrel:** Standard layout altering basic trajectory and range.
**Long Barrel:** Increases range and accuracy at the cost of weight.
**Short Barrel:** Reduces weight and increases aim speed at the cost of range.
**Bull Barrel:** Heavier barrel configuration for increased accuracy and stability.
**Fluted Barrel:** Lightens the weapon while maintaining accuracy and cooling.
**Magnetic Rails:** Electromagnetically accelerates projectiles for massive damage.
**Suppressed Barrel:** Integrates a sound suppressor to mask firing profile.
**Advanced Barrel:** Improves heat mechanics for increased damage at the cost of fire rate.



###### Cover

**Removed Cover:** Exposes internal casing to reduce weapon weight.
**Standard Cover:** Standard housing cover layout.
**Amplifier:** Internal housing energy amplifier to scale energy output.



###### Laser \& Optics

**Iron Sights:** Standard mechanical targeting layout.
**Laser Sight:** Casts a laser tracking node to increase hip-fire accuracy.
**Recon Laser Sight:** Targets marked automatically when aimed over.
**Reflex Sight:** Electronic sight offering rapid target acquisition.
**Holographic Sight:** High-accuracy holographic projection aiming lens.
**Short Scope:** Low-magnification long-range optic.
**Medium Scope:** Mid-magnification long-range optic.
**Long Scope:** High-magnification long-range optic.
**Recon Scope:** High-magnification tracking scope layout.



###### Muzzle

**Compensator:** Increases stability and hip-fire accuracy.
**Muzzle Brake:** Controls gas distribution to stabilize severe vertical recoil.
**Large Muzzle Brake:** Heavy recoil control module.
**Suppressor:** Separately attached muzzle suppressor for stealth optimization.
**Stealth Lasers:** Lowers weapon emission visibility signature.
**Shock Charge Band:** Adds an electrical component to impacts.
**Choke:** Specifically constricts shotgun projectile spread patterns.
**Focus Nozzle:** Pinpoints plasma/laser arcs into highly concentrated beams.



###### Stock \& Grips

**Standard Grip:** Standard grip profile.
**Tactical Grip:** Lightweight ergonomics for increased aim down sights speed.
**Ergonomic Grip:** Highly tailored grip to balance recoil control.
**Standard Stock:** Standard rifle buttstock.
**Tactical Stock:** Lightened stock configuration maximizing handling traits.
**Ergonomic Stock:** Optimal weight allocation to mitigate prolonged sight drift.
**Stabilizing Stock:** Specialized heavy stock to curb automatic recoil sway.
**Folding Stock:** Greatly reduces size and weapon weight.



###### Magazine \& Battery

**Standard Magazine/Battery:** Default structural ammunition cell capacity.
**Small/Large/Tactical Magazine/Battery:** Shifts cell capacity or reload pacing parameters.
**Drum Magazine:** Maximizes storage capacity at the absolute cost of speed.
**Armor-Piercing Rounds:** Heavy-casing projectiles built to defeat armor layers.
**Depleted Uranium Rounds:** Highly dense cores that cut through advanced armor arrays.
**EM-Charged Shot / Electromagnetic Beams/Magazine:** Inflicts systemic disruption to stun organic and mechanical entities.
**Explosive Rounds:** Generates volatile shell detonations upon impact points.
**Flechette Rounds:** Fires tearing barbs that slice through soft target armor layers.
**Penetrator Rounds:** Pierces cleanly through enemies and thin cover surfaces.
**Whitehot Rounds:** Thermal-tipped ammunition designed to continuously burn targets.
**Annihilator Rounds:** Projectiles radiating an unstable aura that aggressively spreads across adjacent entities.

**Slug Shot:** Single, large projectiles that can be fired much further and more accurately.



###### Internal

**Amplifier:** Scales energy beam potency and overall kinetic power output.
**Bullet Hose:** Specialized high-cycle action multiplying range, fire rate, and accuracy attributes.
**Hair Trigger:** Significantly increases weapon cycle response and fire rate.
**High Powered:** Upgrades internal receiver linkages to boost muzzle energy and damage.
**High Velocity:** Specialized internal component optimization to drive higher bullet speeds.
**Overclocked:** Forces mechanical actions past intended safety baselines to elevate cyclic fire rate.



###### Receiver

**Semi-Automatic:** Traditional single projectile discharge per cycle.
**Burst Fire:** Discharges a set, high-velocity grouped burst per single action.
**Fully Automatic:** Provides a continuous payload stream while trigger remains down.
**Binary Trigger:** Doubles fire cycling speeds by firing on both trigger depression and release steps.



###### Melee Additions

**Alloyed Steel Blade:** Hardened edge composition augmenting base cutting power.
**Corroding Blade:** Imbues slashes with persistent acidic armor-stripping properties.
**Alloy Handle:** Lightens balance to favor swift kinetic attack cycles.
**Ergonomic Handle:** Stabilizes stance to maximize weapon impact force profiles.
**Experimental Alloy Handle:** Advanced construction balancing extreme cutting swing paces.
**Accelerant:** Dramatically boosts swinging speed at the absolute cost of edge tracking.



###### Upgrade Module

**Accuracy Upgrade:** Permanent technical application optimizing stability thresholds.
**Aim Speed Upgrade:** Mechanical modifications targeting target acquisition velocity.
**Ammo Capacity Upgrade:** Physical alignment increasing structural ammunition capacities.
**Attack Speed Upgrade:** Mechanical adjustments boosting physical strike or action velocity.
**Damage Upgrade:** Baseline calibration pushing core explosive or physical impact metrics higher.
**Range Upgrade:** Muzzle and energy alignment extending terminal velocity profiles.
**Reload Speed Upgrade:** Friction reduction to optimize magazine exchange windows.
