⚙️ Unlocking and Upgrading Cyberamps

General Details

Each Cyberamp has three Marks — I, II, III — providing +5%, +10%, and +15% bonuses respectively.

Unlock Source

Dr. Sophia Laine at the Neuroamp Clinic (appears after completing the mission "The Stars Have Eyes").

Upgrade Path

Each Mark upgrade requires additional Computer Cores and Watchtower Intel.

Crew Synergy

Equipping Cyberamps on fleet crew stacks their bonuses across all managed ships.

Visual Variants

Available as left- or right-side facial implants. Optional invisible versions can be used via the "Watchtower Internal Cyberamps" mod.
