### **Cyberamps**



Each has three Marks — I, II, III — providing +5 %, +10 %, +15 % bonuses respectively.) 



**CyberAmp						           Function / Buff									Category**

**Lightwave Accelerator**				Increases ship speed and maneuvering thrust.						Mobility

**Ballistic Calibrator**					Boosts ballistic weapon damage.								Weapons

**Precision Module**					Enhances energy weapon damage.								Weapons

**Quark Stabilizer**					Raises particle weapon damage.								Weapons

**Beam Harmonizer**					Improves laser weapon efficiency and reduces heat buildup.			Weapons

**Targeting Computer**					Increases ballistic weapon accuracy and lock‑on speed.				Weapons

**Tactical Jump Interface**				Reduces grav‑jump cooldown and fuel use.						Navigation

**Missile Guidance**					Improves missile tracking and damage.							Weapons

**Disruption Coordinator**				Boosts electromagnetic (EM) weapon damage.						Weapons

**Diagnostic Suite**					Enhances ship repair rate and system recovery speed.				Engineering

**Propulsion Matrix**					Raises top speed and acceleration.								Mobility

**Trajectory Analyzer**					Increases missile damage output.								Weapons

**Magnetic Synchronizer**				Improves reactor efficiency and weapon recharge rate.				Power

**Shield Amplifier**					Expands total shield capacity.								Defense



⚙️ Unlocking and Upgrading

* Unlock source: Dr. Sophia Laine at the Neuroamp Clinic (appears after completing The Stars Have Eyes).
* Upgrade path: Each Mark requires additional Computer Cores and Watchtower Intel.
* Crew synergy: Equipping CyberAmps on fleet crew stacks their bonuses across all managed ships.
* Visual variants: Left‑ or right‑side facial implants; optional “internal” (invisible) versions via Watchtower Internal Cyberamps mod.
